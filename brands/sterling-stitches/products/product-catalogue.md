## Usage Notes

- All prices are in Nigerian Naira (₦). Mid band: ₦100,000–₦160,000. Premium band: ₦160,000–₦350,000. Signature band: ₦350,000–₦550,000. Entry band: under ₦100,000.
- Adire products are artisan-crafted; no two pieces are identical. This uniqueness is a primary editorial differentiator.
- Aso-oke and Agbada pieces carry strong cultural significance in Yoruba heritage — editorial must handle this with authority and care.
- Update status and add new products before each quarterly planning cycle.
