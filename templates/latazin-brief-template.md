# Latazin Brief

## Purpose

This is a pre-generation brief for a single latazin. It captures every decision that must be made before content generation begins: what the latazin argues, who it is for, which products it features, what type it uses, and what effect it should produce in the reader. A completed brief is the required input for latazin generation.

No latazin should be generated without a completed brief. The brief ensures that every piece of content published has a clear editorial reason to exist.

_What is the central tension this latazin holds? Tension is what makes editorial content interesting — it is the gap between what the reader assumes and what the latazin reveals, between what is expected and what is true. State the tension as a contrast or a question._

**Tension:**
