# Annual Editorial Plan

## Purpose

This is a working document for planning a full year of editorial operations within the Latazin Editorial Operating System. It captures the annual thesis, business priorities, quarterly arcs, audience and product emphasis, content balance, and review cadence. A team fills this in at the start of each planning year and revisits it at mid-year.

This template produces the document that the quarterly planning process depends on. Every quarterly plan, monthly calendar, and individual latazin brief should trace back to decisions made here.

---

## How This File Is Used in the System

```
House Documents (voice, typology, style, rubric, metaphors, page roles)
         +
Input Documents (catalogue, collections, personas, occasions, months, performance log)
         │

  ┌──────────────────────────┐

  └──────────────────────────┘
         │
```

Complete this document before beginning any quarterly planning. Revisit and revise at mid-year based on performance data and shifting priorities.
