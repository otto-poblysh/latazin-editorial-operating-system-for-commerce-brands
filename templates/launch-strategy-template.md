# Launch Strategy

## Purpose

This is a strategy and checklist template for launching a Latazin-powered editorial website or campaign. It covers everything from pre-launch content readiness to post-launch review: ensuring house documents are populated, the content pipeline is primed, channels are mapped, audiences are targeted, and success is defined before the first latazin goes live.

Use this template when launching the Latazin Editorial Operating System for a new brand, launching a new editorial destination, or executing a major campaign that requires coordinated multi-channel rollout.
